Ehsaas Program Abbreviation - 8171 Ehsaas Program 25000 BISP

Introduction

The Government of Pakistan has introduced the 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP 25000, a vital social protection initiative designed to provide financial assistance to families in need. Launched under the leadership of Imran Khan’s government, this program aims to uplift vulnerable communities by reducing economic inequality and making Pakistan a social welfare state.

What is the 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP?

The 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP offers financial aid to eligible families, with stipends ranging from Rs. 12,000 to Rs. 25,000. It builds upon the foundations laid by the Benazir Income Support Program (BISP) of 2009, reflecting the government’s commitment to supporting widows, students, unemployed individuals, and low-income families.

Objectives of the 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP

The Ehsaas Program Abbreviation stands for a broad social safety initiative aimed at reducing poverty and supporting vulnerable communities across Pakistan. "Ehsaas," meaning "compassion" in Urdu, reflects the program's mission to provide financial assistance to those in need. The Ehsaas Program abbreviation is associated with various schemes like Ehsaas Emergency Cash and Ehsaas Kafaalat, designed to address economic disparities and promote social welfare. By targeting low-income families, orphans, widows, and the disabled, the program aims to create a more inclusive and equitable society in Pakistan.

  • Reducing inequality and improving the quality of life for deserving families.
  • Providing financial stability to those in need.
  • Empowering women and supporting low-income households.

Eligibility Criteria for 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP

To apply for the 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP, applicants must meet specific criteria. The eligibility criteria include:

  • Having a monthly income of less than Rs. 25,000.
  • Being already registered with the Benazir Income Support Program (BISP).
  • Having at least one female BISP member in the household.
  • Possessing a valid CNIC (Computerized National Identity Card).
  • Being affected by a disaster, pandemic, or other emergencies.
  • Having a bank account to receive the monthly stipend.

Online Registration Process

The registration process for the 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP is simple and can be completed online. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  • Visit the official BISP website and navigate to the registration section.
  • Enter your CNIC number and contact information.
  • Provide personal details such as date of birth, marital status, and family information.
  • Submit your bank account details for the monthly payments.
  • Double-check all the information and submit your application.

Tracking Application Status

To check your eligibility or track your application status, you can:

  • Send your CNIC number via SMS to 8171.
  • Use the Raabta app, available on the Google Play Store.
  • Visit the official Ehsaas Program portal.

8171 Ehsaas Program BISP Latest Update 2024

As of 2024, beneficiaries of the 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP will continue to receive their stipends. From January 1st, 2024, an amount of Rs. 8,500 will be transferred to eligible individuals. Additionally, under the Kafaalat program, 15,000 children will receive Taleemi Wazifa of Rs. 20,000 each.

How to Withdraw Cash from an ATM

Withdrawing your Ehsaas Program stipend from an ATM is easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Insert your card into the ATM.
  2. Select the "Cash Withdrawal" option from the screen.
  3. Choose the amount you wish to withdraw.
  4. Confirm the transaction and collect your cash.
  5. Don’t forget to take your card!

Conclusion

The 8171 Ehsaas Program BISP is a significant step towards social welfare and economic stability in Pakistan. By providing financial support to those who need it the most, the program aims to bridge the gap between the rich and poor, empower women, and uplift low-income families. If you meet the eligibility criteria, don’t hesitate to register and take advantage of this valuable program.

For more information, visit the official Ehsaas Program Portal.
